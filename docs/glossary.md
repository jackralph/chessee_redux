# Glossary of terms

## Abandonment Move

A `move` which leaves the king in check.

## Continual Piece

A continual `piece` is one which can continue attacking through one of it's own pieces (as a form of backup), examples of continual pieces are:

- diagonal
  - `"bishop"`
  - `"queen"`
- straight
  - `"queen"`
  - `"rook"`
